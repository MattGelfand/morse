Crafting Beautiful Interfaces For Web, Mobile and Desktop

Matt Gelfand

I devise, design and occasionally develop smart and accessibile user experiences. A writer in a former life, I earned a master's degree from Northwestern's Medill School of Journalism where I specialized in interactive publishing, subsequently learning to code in the process. I cut my teeth at a software lab helping to design apps to make journalist's lives' easier. I then shifted my focus to UX at an agency that's been a pioneer of the discipline for nearly a decade. Give me a project I'm passionate about, and I will relentlessly tinker until both the design and code are sound and aesthetically pleasing. I believe in building hastily and failing early, because the best ideas are never your first. Other mantras: Steal like an artist, don't make users think, and always have a plan - without one you have no destination, which means you can never arrive.
