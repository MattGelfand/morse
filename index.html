Somebackground:

I devise, design and occasionally develop smart and accessibile user experiences. I own a master's degree in interactive publishing from Northwestern's Medill School, and currently work as UX Designer at Fresh Tilled Soil, a firm that's been a pioneer of the user experience discipline for more than a decade. I believe in building hastily and failing early, because the best ideas are never your first. Other mantras: Steal like an artist, don't make users think, and always have a plan - without one you have no destination, which means you can never arrive.
